Saina Nehwal is the first Indian badminton player to ever win an Olympic medal. When the Indian shuttler won the bronze medal at the London 2012 Games, she made history. When the Haryana shuttler won the BWF World Junior Championships in 2008, she gained attention pretty early in her career. Saina has won 23 singles championships in total.

Bachendri Pal

Bachendri Pal set a record on May 23, 1984, when she conquered Mount Everest, the highest mountain peak in the world. A day before turning thirteen, Bachendri made India proud by being the first female climber to climb Everest. With desire in her heart , she enrolled in the Nehru Institute of Mountaineering to study the ropes of climbing.

Lata Mangeshkar

Lata Mangeshkar furnished the singing voice for a plethora of Bollywood leading women over the course of a career that lasted close to eight decades. She provided the vocals for more than 5,000 songs in more than 1,000 Hindi and 36 regional films. She had an unmatched influence on Indian film music and was regarded as having one of the most diverse voices during the boom of Hindi film.

Offline Events

Embroidery Camp

(Organised in Mumbai on 20th August 2022)

According to tradition, embroidery is a 500-year-old quilting art that is mostly practised by women. Many women who were formerly connected with embroidery to make a  living. They are now are becoming more and more interested in needlework. For the same reason, we set up an embroidery camp where visitors could either learn the skill or show off and sell their accomplished work. Women are delighted for more reasons than financial gain because they see their employment as an "Izzat wala kaam (a respectable career)".

Self-defense

(Organised in New Delhi on 15th August 2022)

It's an unpleasant part of life that sometimes you may need to defend your family or yourself from an outside attack. One of the many reasons why mastering self-defense techniques could be so vital is because women are more susceptible to attacks of any type. Because of this, our group frequently organizes self-defense camps. Also It has been observed that Women have advantages from learning self-defense techniques that go beyond just learning how to defend oneself. Additionally, it improves their personalities and self-esteem.

Get-togethers Kitty Party

(Organised in Chandigarh on 1st September 2022)

The kitty party is a rotating savings organisation in which member women contribute a certain amount to the pool, or kitty, at each meeting. The host receives the money collected; each member must host the kitty once every round. We also here host kitty parties (or get-togethers) on occasion to have fun and enjoy ourselves with a bunch of ladies who come out of their hectic schedules, leaving all their problems at home.
